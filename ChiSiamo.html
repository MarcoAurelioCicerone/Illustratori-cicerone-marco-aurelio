Chi Siamo

We’re a global collaboration agency representing a hand-picked group of artists working across a wide range of disciplines, from 3D and animation to illustration and typography.

Founded in 2007, our offices are based in Helsinki, Amsterdam, Los Angeles and New York.

Our artists are highly admired by their peers, with styles as diverse as they are distinctive.

Representing both established names and the very best newcomers from around the world, our roster celebrates diversity and unique artistic expression.

Building close and long-lasting relationships throughout each project we take on, we act as a trusted partner for our artists and a reliable resource for our clients.

We’re represented in China by our local partner, V/Collective.
